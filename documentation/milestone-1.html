Purpose

  • This project will be a professional portfolio website showcasing my experiences through creative applications of computer science.

Audience

  • This website is designed for a professional audience, with an added creative flair to be more eye-catching. The target audience includes hiring managers, recruiters, and peers.

Goal

  • The primary goal of this website is to showcase my work and provide a point of contact for potential employers or collaborators through the contact form or linked social media profiles.
